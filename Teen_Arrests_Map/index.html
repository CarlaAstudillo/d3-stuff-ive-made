Rio de Janeiro Detention of Minors by Police

The map below shows the total number of adolescents that have been detained by police in each neighborhood of Rio de Janeiro from Jan. 2015 until Feb. 2016. The second number includes the number of minors that enter the juvenile justice system which includes confinement and imprisonment in juvenile detention centers which have been criticized by Human Rights Watch as overcrowded, abusive and inhumane.
